Kennedy Bailey: Biography

Kennedy Bailey was born in Manchester, Connecticut in 1997. He grew up in Mansfield, Connecticut. He has an older sister, Shannon, who studies physical therapy at Boston University. He attended E.O. Smith High School, where he ran Cross Country, sang, and made music and art. He currently attends Tufts University and is studying Computer Science and also upholding his musical and artistic hobbies.
