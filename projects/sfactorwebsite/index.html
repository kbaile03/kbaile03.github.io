About Us

In the fall of 2006, five Tufts students, Theron Corbin '08, Matt Johnson '09, Ted Boughter-Dornfeld '08, Alexi Paraschos '09, and Ikenna Acholonu '10, came together to form S-Factor. Citing a lack of available outlets to express their brand of musical expression, namely music of the African diaspora, these students resolved to create a group from which they could create and share art not only amongst themselves, but with the greater Tufts community. Since then, S-Factor has become a brotherhood of students from all walks of life striving to create both meaning and quality entertainment through song. Their music encompasses, but is not limited to Gospel, Soul, R&B, Jazz, Funk, Hip Hop, Spirituals, Rap, & Reggae. S-Factor released its first self-titled album Soul Factor in 2010 and has been busy performing shows on its Medford campus as well as various venues throughout Boston, Chicago, and New York City. As a service-oriented group on the up & coming, S-Factor is always ready to share their talents with the world. As the lyrics to their signature standard "One More Time" states:

"Behold how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity."
